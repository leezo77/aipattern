Core Concepts

Patterns

Reusable, high-level guides for solving common problems. Patterns describe the general shape of a solution without dictating specific technical details.

Blueprints

Detailed, end-to-end designs for a unique solution to a specific business problem. The master architectural document defining the 'what' and 'why'.

Playbooks

Dynamic, operational guides with step-by-step instructions and SOPs for day-to-day execution. The 'how-to' guide for operational teams.
